Project Meridian: Meridian Compliance Software, Inc.

Business overview

Meridian Compliance Software provides workflow and audit-trail software for mid-market
manufacturers subject to environmental and safety reporting mandates. The company serves
214 customers across North America. Revenue for fiscal year 2025 was $61.8 million, up
14.0 percent from $54.2 million in fiscal year 2024. Approximately 86 percent of revenue
is recurring subscription revenue billed annually in advance.

Reported EBITDA for fiscal year 2025 was $12.1 million, a 19.6 percent margin. Management
believes normalized EBITDA is materially higher after adjusting for owner compensation and
one-time system implementation costs incurred during the year.

The company employs 292 full-time staff, of whom 96 are in engineering and product. The
founder-CEO owns 71 percent of fully diluted equity; a family trust holds the remainder.

Transaction context

The founder is seeking a majority recapitalization to fund succession and continued product
investment. Management has proposed a transaction perimeter that excludes the company's
small services subsidiary in Mexico, which generated $1.9 million of revenue in fiscal 2025.
